About Me

Under the supervision of Prof. Ryan Cloutier, I currently study the elemental abundances of M Dwarfs at McMaster University. I previously graduated with a Bachelor's degree in astronomy, physics and chemistry from the University of Toronto Mississauga. In my spare time, I love to play video games, do crosswords, and fawn over cats on the internet.

If you have any questions about me or my research, feel free to shoot me an email at gromekn@mcmaster.ca.

M Dwarf Compositions

I am using SPIRou spectra of co-evolved FGK-M binary star systems to calibrate previously unreliable measurements of elemental abundances derived from M dwarf spectra. We can then apply the calibration to planet-hosting M dwarfs and constrain planet formation models.

Cygnus Loop

A study of the Magnetic field structure and morphology of the famous Cygnus Loop Supernova Remnant.
